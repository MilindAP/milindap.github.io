## About Me

I am Milind Patil, a researcher working at the intersection of **AI/ML**, **Wireless Communications**, and **CSI-based Sensing**.

I hold an M.S. in Electrical Engineering from **San Jose State University** and worked as a **Cellular Engineer** at Apple (via OSI Engineering) focusing on RF desense, wireless system evaluation, and test automation.

## Research Interests

- CSI-based Human Activity Recognition  
- AI/ML for Wireless Communications  
- mmWave/6G PHY Layer Modeling  
- Multimodal AI (CSI + Vision)  
- Signal Processing & RF Systems
