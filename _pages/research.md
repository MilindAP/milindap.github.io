# Research Areas

### CSI-based Human Activity Recognition
Deep learning & LLM-based fusion of wireless CSI and vision for fall detection and HAR.

### AI/ML for Wireless Communications
LSTMs, LTC networks, Transformers, and Symbolic Regression for wireless prediction and interpretation.

### mmWave & 6G Modeling
Beamforming, multi-link prediction, and sub-THz modeling for next-gen networks.

### Multimodal Sensing
Fusion of CSI features and vision data using VLM/LLM architectures.
